How It Works

Each node is a person in a 3D grid. Neighbors = close contacts (6 per person).

R₀ > 1 = epidemic explodes. R₀ < 1 = infection dies out.

The 2/π compression is the natural recovery rate. When contagiousness (α) overcomes recovery, the infection spreads at 1 hop/day — the "speed of light" for disease spread.

Watch the diamond wavefront — that's the infection expanding through the population in real time.
